The Problem

Tracking progress shouldn't feel like a second job

Workouts, meals, weight, macros — spread across several apps, with manual data entry that gets skipped the moment training gets busy.

WITHOUT FITNESS AGENT

  • Separate apps for workouts, calories, and progress photos
  • Manual food searches that rarely match what you actually ate
  • No one connects your training data to your nutrition data
  • Generic advice, not based on your own history

WITH FITNESS AGENT

  • One chat for logging training and food, in your own words
  • Automatic matching against a 7,140-item German food database
  • Progress and nutrition feed the same coaching engine
  • Feedback grounded in peer-reviewed sports-science sources
How it works

From a sentence to science-based feedback

01

You log naturally

"Yesterday bench press 80kg 3x8" or "I had 200g chicken and rice."

02

Agent matches the facts

Food is matched to the BLS nutrition database; workouts are stored with full history.

03

Science gets pulled in

A curated knowledge base (RAG) supplies the relevant training or nutrition guideline.

04

You get real coaching

The LLM combines your data with the science — no hardcoded thresholds, no generic advice.

Features

Everything a strength athlete needs to track

Training

Log it, track it, beat it

  • Natural language workout logging
  • Personal records & automatic plateau detection
  • Weekly training volume, per exercise or overall
  • 1RM estimation (Epley formula)
  • Plausibility checks catch obvious typos
Nutrition

Real food data, not guesswork

  • Matched against the official BLS food database (~7,140 items)
  • Automatic portion size estimation
  • Smart disambiguation — only asks when truly ambiguous
  • Daily & weekly overview with custom date ranges
  • Correct past entries directly in the chat
Coaching

Feedback grounded in evidence

  • Automatic feedback after logging, on training and nutrition
  • Cites the underlying guideline (e.g. ISSN, WHO, Schoenfeld)
  • Reminds you that progress is a weekly average, not one day
Profile & Progress

Your history, always in view

  • Persistent profile & weight history
  • Goal history — every change recorded, never overwritten
  • Charts for weight, exercise progression, and volume
Design principle: facts come from the database, thresholds come from the RAG knowledge base, and the LLM combines both to write the actual feedback — no hardcoded nutrition or training numbers in the code.
